GAME DESIGN & PRODUCTION
The Forbidden Palace
The Forbidden Palace is a horror based game, which is inspired from the palace "The Forbidden City" at Beijing, China.
We were a group of 5 designers/developers and designed the game from pre to post productions.
The main concept was to let the user wear a oximeter, and the heart rate measured through the oximeter will determine the behavior of the ghosts and their movement patterns. I acted as the team lead & game designer and conducted qualitative UX research such as SWOT, Competative analysis and researched the game market.

Skills
UX Research, Game design, Game development, Unity Environment

Tools
Maya 3D, Unity, C#, UX Research, Excel

Platform
Windows
