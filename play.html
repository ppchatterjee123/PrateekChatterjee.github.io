GAME DESIGN & PRODUCTION
The Forbidden Palace
"The Forbidden Palace" is a horror game that draws inspiration from Beijing's historic "Forbidden City."
Our team included five game designers and developers who worked together from the initial stages to the final production. The central idea behind the game was to immerse players by incorporating an oximeter that measures their heart rate, which in turn affects the behavior and movement patterns of the ghosts within the game.
I had the privilege of leading the team and spearheading the brainstorming sessions for the game's concept, gameplay mechanics, and unique features. Additionally, I conducted qualitative user experience research, including SWOT analysis and competitive analysis, while also utilizing Unity to design the game environment.

Skills
Game design, UX Research, Game development, Unity

Tools
Maya 3D, Unity, C#, UX Research, Excel

Platform
Windows
