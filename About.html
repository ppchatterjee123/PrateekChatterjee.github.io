About My Work
I am a UX Designer currently working at Softrams in Washington DC - Baltimore area. In 2015, I graduated with a Bachelor in Engineering (CS) from Mumbai University, India. I moved to the US in 2016 and got the oppotuinity to research & specialize in the Human Computer Interaction field.

For the past two years, I've had the opportunity to lead the UX research & design for two products. One of them is ACO-MS, a federal agency healthcare product, which is within the United States Department of Health and Human Services and other being H-Drive, which is a Healthcare SaaS product. My goal was to deliver intuitive yet robust products that create a enjoyable experience for users & provide growth and success for the business.

According to the latest performance data for the ACO-MS product (Federal Agency Product), Accountable care organizations (ACO Users) had a record year in 2020. ACO Users saved $1.2 billion in Medicare in 2019 as compared to $739 million in 2018. (More Info)
