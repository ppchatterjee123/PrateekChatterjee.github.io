About My Work

I moved to the US in 2016 and got the oppotuinity to pursue MS in Interactive Media & Game Development at WPI where I specialized in the Human Computer Interaction. For the past year at CGI, I've helped clients in Telecom, Healthcare, AI (Hyper-automation & Decision making), VR and Automotive industries. Recently we were able to successfully migrate Lumen and Century link webpages into Brightspeed Business experience.

I've had the opportunity to lead the UX research & design for two products at Softrams. One of them is ACO-MS, a federal agency healthcare product, which is within the United States Department of Health and Human Services and other being H-Drive, which is a Healthcare SaaS product. My goal was to deliver intuitive yet robust products that create an enjoyable experience for users & provide growth and success for the business.
