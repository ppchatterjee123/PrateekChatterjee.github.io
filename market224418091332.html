BACKGROUND
Hdrive Marketplace is an android/iOS based app used for checking the drug coverage from a particular plan ID or using the Zipcode to check if the drugs are covered in the particular plan or not.
ROLE
I was the sole UX designer of the team and carried out qualitative analysis for the application, while the researcher carried out the Quantitative part of the project.
TOOLS
Figma, Miro, Jira
QUALITATIVE RESEARCH
I started to analyze similar products and collected data regarding the good and bad of other products. The researcher started analyzing and creating the User Flow for the application as the business goals were defined by the Product manager and CTO.
